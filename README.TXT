Step 2: When the download is finish. Right click the zip archieve and click Extract files or Extract Here.
Step 3: After extracting the files, copy the file and paste in the directory in the wamp then open the folder of the course website.
Step 4: Open index.html and enjoy the course website of the group.
Step 5: Click on the prelim for the prelim notes
Step 6: Click on the midterm for the midterm notes
